Brightleaf Retail — Customer FAQ

Q: What is Brightleaf's return policy?
A: Brightleaf accepts returns within 15 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Furniture items have a shorter 7-day return window due to assembly considerations.

Q: Does Brightleaf offer cash on delivery?
A: Cash on delivery is available for orders under Rs. 10,000 in the 25 cities where Brightleaf currently offers same-day delivery. Orders above this amount require prepayment.

Q: How can I track my AquaPure X200 warranty claim?
A: Warranty claims for AquaPure products purchased through Brightleaf can be tracked using the order ID through the Brightleaf app under 'My Orders' > 'Warranty Status'.
